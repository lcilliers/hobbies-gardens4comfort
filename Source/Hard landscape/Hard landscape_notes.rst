Hard landscape
================
2024-02-15: Time for some maintenance.  The Patio and two sheds got a good wash down and repainting, with the help of Grant.

2002-05-09: I installed all the garden hoses now successfully in a position where it can reach a large part of the garden, and remain in a permanent position - with much greater ease of operation. It is especially the large hose in the woodlands that is very heavy, and difficult to handle. One day I will replace it with a much lighter version.
2002-03-26: At last I installed the garden hose agaist the wall at the garage to make it easier to extract and to roll up again. Hopefully it will look neater and not like an eye sore.
2000-12-24: We built a garden path to the greenhouse and through the garden work area today. It became so muddy, and unpleasant to work there.  It is really looking very good now.
2000-11-06: I finished building the wooden arch as the entrance to the scented garden and redressed the roses over it. I am really looking forward to  the white display next year.
