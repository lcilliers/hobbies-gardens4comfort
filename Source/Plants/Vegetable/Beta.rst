Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Vegetable  - Propagation:  Seed - Hardiness:  half hardy - Flowering Season:  

Sow March to July directly in sunny site. can also be sown in modules and planted out after all risk of frost has passed.

When harvesting, twist the leaves from the root to prevent staining.
