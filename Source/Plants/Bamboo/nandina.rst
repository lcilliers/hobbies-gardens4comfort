nandina
=========
               
Heavenly bamboo, Sacred bamboo, Nandina
-----------------------------------------
This is a versatile shrub providing all year-round interest.  The leaves are a bronzy-pink, and turn light green, then to bronze and crimson in the autumn. The flowers are white and bloom in clusters in mid summer, followed by red berries.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Heavenly bamboo, Sacred bamboo, Nandina  - Family: domestica  - Variety:
