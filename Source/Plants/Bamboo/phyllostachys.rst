phyllostachys
===============
               
zigzag bamboo
---------------
called the Zigzag bamboo, it is clump-forming with slightly ribbed, slender, arching canes, bright green at first, turning yellow-brown to almost black with age.  Leaves retain their colour throughout the winter.  2-10m* indefinite. 

Full hardy
