Sanvitalia
============
               
Creeping zinnia
-----------------
Sanvitalia procumbens with the common name of Mexican creeping zinnia is the type species of the genus Sanvitalia and a member of the family Asteraceae and tribe Heliantheae

Attract bees

good for hanging baskets

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Creeping zinnia  - Family: Asteraceae  - Variety: procumbens

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: annual  - Propagation: Seed  - Hardiness:   - Flowering Season:  
After germination, the plants continue to be cultivated brightly, airily and still somewhat cool. About eight to ten weeks after sowing, Sanvitalia procumbens begins to flower.
