cosmos
========

mix of soft shades, flowers earlier than others, very versatile. Use as filler in border or centrepiece. Long lasting as cutting flower
A tall, free-flowering annual with very fine, fern-like foliage and saucer-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: annual 
 - Propagation: Seed 
 - Hardiness: Tender H7 - H10 (+0) 
 - Flowering Season: Summer to autumn 

2024-03-17 : sow Sonara mixed variety from Dobbies in pods

2023:sow in seed compost, lightly cover. Place in warmth 15-20c; keep moist. Transplant into trays. 20cm between plants

2023-03-04: Sow Cosmos in pods indoors. Cosmos Bipinnatus variety from RHS seedback
