matthiola
===========
               
stock
-------

These are annuals, biennials, perennials and evergreen sub-shrubs, there are about 55 species, they will produce both single and double flowers. the leaves are grey-green in colour. They are Fully hardy to frost tender, with a min of 4C. You should grow in sun or semi-shade with fertile, well drained lime-rich soil. You can propagate sub-shrubs by semi-ripe cuttings in the summer.
