Antirrhinum
=============
               
snapdragon; tom thumb
-----------------------

These are bushy short-lived perennials which are grown for their showy flowers that are borne from spring to autumn. This plant is often found in the wild in Northern Europe.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: snapdragon; tom thumb 
 - Family: Plantaginaceae 
 - Variety: Majus

 - Common Names: Antirrhinum braun-blanquetii 
 - Family: Scrophulariaceae 
 - Variety: braun-blanquetii
