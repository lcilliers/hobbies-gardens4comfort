Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: annual 
 - Propagation: Seed 
 - Hardiness: Hardy H5-H1 (<-10) 
 - Flowering Season: Mid summer 

Succession sowing from febr - april will ensure colour all summer. Sow in propagator febr-march. 15-20c. Transplant when able to handle. Plant out after climatising. Sept sowing - overwinter under glass.  Sow direct april-may.
