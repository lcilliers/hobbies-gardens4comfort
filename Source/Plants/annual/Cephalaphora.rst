Cephalaphora Aromatica Pineapple plant
----------------------------------------
This unusual looking annual is a delightful border plant with bright yellow pom pom flowers that stand on errect stems over mid green foliage. 
 
The flowers are very aromatic with a distinctive fruity aroma when brushed agains
height 30-38cm
light well drained soil in full sun

deadhead to prolonggrowth
