michaelmas daisy, Frost flower
--------------------------------

This plant was grown from a cutting of the previous year, and planted in a container in 1/2 sun. It did very well throughout the summer and flowered well into autumn until first frost

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: michaelmas daisy, Frost flower 
 - Family: michelii
