Nasturtium
------------

Nasturtiums are edible flowers like marigolds, so not only do they look really colourful but they taste good too
look at the seed packet to find out if the variety you have chosen is a bush or climbing variety.

+--------------------------------------------------+
|.. figure:: /Images/Image435.jpg                  |
|   :width: 250px                                  |
|                                                  |
|   DSC01586.jpg: 2020-11-14                       |
|                                                  |
|   nasturtium red                                 |
+--------------------------------------------------+

 - Plant Type: annual 
 - Propagation: Seed 
 - Hardiness: Semi Hardy H6 (-10) 
 - Flowering Season: Summer to autumn 

Nasturtiums like to grow in the sun in well drained soil. Sow from March to May, outside where they are to flower. They will flower from the summer through to the autumn.
