coriander

Coriander lemon flavour
-------------------------
Lemon taste and fragrance.  grow in sunny position.  Can be grown as cut and come again salad leaves. harvest seed when flower heads turn brown. hang stems upside down indoors.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Coriander lemon flavour  - Family:   - Variety: 

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: annual  - Propagation: Seed  - Hardiness: Tender H7 - H10 (+0)  - Flowering Season: Summer to autumn 
Sow April - june thinly directly into fine raked moist soil.  Germinate 7-10 days.
2023-02-25: sow corriander seed in pods indoors. seed is about three years old. have not yet been successfull with previous sowings of this seed.
