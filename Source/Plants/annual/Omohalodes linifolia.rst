Omohalodes linifolia
======================
               
Venus navelwort or argentine forget me not
--------------------------------------------
Hardy annual, height up to 40cm. Copes with drought and alkaline soils.
Fast growing alphine with grey-green leaves on upright branching stems.  Produces little white flowers throughout the summer. looks like forget me nots
