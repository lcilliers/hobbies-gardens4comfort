tagetes
=========
               
marigold tagetes
------------------
Tagetes or marigold have a needle like seed.
performs well, whatever the weather.
Ideal for mass planting
leaves and petals are edible
Many varieties avaliable.  Some of the varieties grown and sown over the years include Naughty marietta, French dwarf double

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: marigold tagetes  - Family: naughty marietta  - Variety: 

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: annual  - Propagation: Seed  - Hardiness: Tender H7 - H10 (+0)  - Flowering Season: Summer to autumn 
Sow indoors in seed trays
allow 20cm between plants
