sorbus
========
               
mountain ash, whitebeam
-------------------------
A graceful and rather slender tree. It has one outstanding characteristic - season-long colour. The S. Aucuparia has a splash of white-creamy flowers opening in May and June, and a display of red berries in late summer. The leaves turn gold in autumn

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: mountain ash, whitebeam  - Family: Aucuparia  - Variety:
