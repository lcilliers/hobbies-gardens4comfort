populus
=========
               
Italian poplar, Canadian poplar, Poplar
-----------------------------------------
This genus consists of about 35 species of fast-growing deciduous trees.  The catkin flowers are borne on separate trees. They are fully hardy.  They prefer sun and will need deep fertile, moist but well drained soil. You can propagate by hardwood cuttings in the winter.
A conical to columnar, deciduous tree of Garden origin. Bears triangular ovate, pointed, glossy bright green leaves. Catkins of yellow-green flowers appear in mid spring.
