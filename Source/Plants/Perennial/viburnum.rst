viburnum
----------
Viburnum plicatum 'Mariesii' Tiers of horizontal branches covered in white lace-cap flowers during early summer.  Deciduous though the layers of bare branches are distinctive even in winter.  The leaves turn a deep read in autumn.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: viburnum  - Family: Plicatum  - Variety:
