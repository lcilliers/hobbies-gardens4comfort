coneflower
------------
Toto has similar flowers to other Rudbeckias, but is far more compact and bushy. It makes excellent bedding plants, but performs equally well in containers.  

Golden yellow petals with dark brown central cone.  Strong growing plant that flowers well into October. Flowers June to first frost.

20-25cm*25cm

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: coneflower  - Family: hirta (toto)  - Variety:
