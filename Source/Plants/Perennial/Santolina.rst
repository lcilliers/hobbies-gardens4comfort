Lavender cotton
-----------------
Chamaecyparissus africanus alternative name.
S. chamaecyparissus is a dwarf evergreen shrub with narrow, silvery-woolly, much dissected leaves and long-stalked, button-like bright yellow flowerheads 2cm in widt

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Lavender cotton  - Family: Asteraceae  - Variety: Tomentosa
