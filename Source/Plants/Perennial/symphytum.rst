symphytum
===========
           
comfrey
---------


Clump forming, hairy, rhizomatous perennial with rosettes of oblong-lance shaped mid-green leaves. Bears bright blue flowers from early to late summer.  60*60cm. 
Propagate by division in spring. Grow in moist, moderately fertile soil in full sun or partial shade. May be invasive. 
Fully hardy.

 - Family: Caucasicum 'wisley blue'
