choisya
=========
               
Mexican orange
----------------
Although all the pictures are from the one main shrub, I have now cultivated several other and planted them in different places in the garden. They grow quickly, is quite shade tolerant, and I expect great things of them. I would like to get the yellow 'sundance' variety.
