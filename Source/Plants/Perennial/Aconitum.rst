aconitum
==========
               
monkshood
-----------
hardy perennial. Full sun, needs staking

Aconitum can be herbaceous perennials or biennials with lobed leaves and racemes or panicles of showy, hooded flowers

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: monkshood  - Family: Ranunculaceae  - Variety: carmichaelii var wilsonii

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Perennial  - Propagation: Division  - Hardiness: Tender H7 - H10 (+0)  - Flowering Season: Summer to autumn 
Seed. Fl late summer-autumn. 120-180cm. An imposing, long flowering long lived perennial with pyramids of beautiful 5cm flowers. Pre-chill seed for 6-8 weeks.

Divide every third year in autumn or late winter to maintain vigour, although plants can be slow to re-establish. Use gloves when handling plants as all parts are highly toxic
