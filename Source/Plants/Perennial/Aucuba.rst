aucuba
========
               
spotted laurel
----------------
Evergreen dioecious shrub. Most valued for there tolerance for full shade, dry soils. pollution and salt winds.  Use as specimen plants, for hedges and screens, or to fill a dark corner where little else will flourish. Also suitable for containers and as large houseplants.  Female plant produce red berries.
