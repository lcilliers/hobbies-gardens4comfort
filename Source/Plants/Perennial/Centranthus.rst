Valerian
----------
this perenial grows easily and wild and has light and dark pink, and White flowers. it seeds itself.  it bears sweetly scented pink or white flowers that attract many fly species, especially hoverflies.Crude extract of valerian root may have sedative and anxiolytic effects, and is commonly sold in dietary supplement capsules to promote sleep
