Erigeron
==========
               
Erigeron Mexican fleabane
---------------------------
Daisy like flower with a hint of pink.  Can flower for about 9 months.  Gently self sowing into noons and cracks.  Perfect for walls and paths

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Erigeron Mexican fleabane  - Family: karvinskianus  - Variety:
