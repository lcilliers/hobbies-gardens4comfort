firethorn
-----------
This vigorous, evergreen, bushy shrub has white flowers, borne in clusters, in early summer, with oval, dark green leaves.  Rounded, orange-red fruit then appears in early autumn.

firethorne
------------
This species is upright and will spread with long arching branches, It will also produce fragrant blooms in the spring and then bright red berries in the late summer and autumn.
Put photo in from bush at back door
