Francoa
=========
Wedding flower Francoa sonchifolia
------------------------------------An evergreen perennial with a rosette of lobed leaves and racemes of dark-veined, pale pink flowers, in summer
Also known as bridal Wreath

Classification
----------------
 - Common Names: Wedding flower Francoa sonchifolia  - Family:   - Variety: sonchifolia; ramosa
