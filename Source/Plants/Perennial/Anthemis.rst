Golden marguerite
-------------------
A clump-forming, free-flowering perennial with inversely lance-shaped to obovate, 2-3-pinnatisect, mid-green, basal leaves, 1-5cm long and smaller stem leaves.   In the summer, branching stems produce solitary, golden yellow to cream flowers.
May need staking. Cut to ground level after flowering for good leaf rosettes in winter.
