bellflower Persicifolia
-------------------------

This perennial will produce medium green lance-shaped leaves and pretty clusters of bell-shaped, delicate flowers with papery petals which will form a bell-shape. The foliage will form a basal rosette.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: bellflower Persicifolia 
 - Family: Campanulaceae 
 - Variety: Persicifolia

Clustered bellflower glomerata
--------------------------------

Campanula glomerata makes excellent cut flowers that last up to 2 weeks in a vase! Easy to grow, virtually disease and pest free, this low care perennial is also deer and rabbit resistant and adored by hummingbirds, butterflies and bees.

 - Common Names: Clustered bellflower glomerata 
 - Family: Campanulaceae 
 - Variety: glomerata

harebell bellflower blue ice
------------------------------

hardy perennial. Seed. 30cm. Excellent in front border. Rich. Fertile soil in full sun. flowers summer.
A beautiful and long-lasting plant for the front of the border or the rockery

 - Common Names: harebell bellflower blue ice 
 - Family: blue ice 
 - Variety: 

+--------------------------------------------------+
|.. figure:: /Images/Image2316.jpg                 |
|   :width: 250px                                  |
|                                                  |
|   campanula harebell.jpg: 2021-02-14             |
|                                                  |
|   harebell                                       |
+--------------------------------------------------+

sow seed at 15-20c on surface of damp seed compost. Do not cover seed. Place in propagator until after germination, 21-30d. Do not exclude light.
Transplant into pots. Plant 60cm apart.
Alternatively sow late summer in cool greenhouse and overwinter plants in cool, well lit conditions. Plant out in spring. This produces larger plants.
               
Campanula trailing bellfower poscharskyana
--------------------------------------------

C. poscharskyana is a spreading perennial forming a low mound of rounded leaves, with spreading stems bearing star-shaped light violet-blue flowers in summer and early autumn

 - Common Names: Campanula trailing bellfower poscharskyana 
 - Family: Campanulaceae 
 - Variety: poscharskyana
