laurelia
==========
               
Chilean laurel
----------------
The leaves are 3to7cm, elliptical to oblong or obovate, with entire or serrated margins, which have an upper glaucescent surface.  The flowers are racemes about 2.5cm long with the perianths being silky and pubescent. Very aromatic leaves. Axillary of tiny cub shaped green flowers produced in early summer.
