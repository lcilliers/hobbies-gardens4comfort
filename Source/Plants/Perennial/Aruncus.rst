Aruncus
=========
               
White goat's beard Aruncus
----------------------------

Aruncus, also known as Brides Feathers with large leaves with white flowers turning yellow in autumn. Forms dense and bushy clump. Flowers June, July. Soil to remain moist, partial shade. 90-120 cm. Dies back in winter. Attract bees, butterflies and month. RHS award.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: White goat's beard Aruncus 
 - Family: Rosaceae 
 - Variety: Diocus

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Perennial 
 - Propagation: Seed 
 - Hardiness: Hardy H5-H1 (<-10) 
 - Flowering Season: Mid summer 

Germinate best after cold period.  Barely cover, keep moist. Germination is eratic. keep seedlings out of direct sunlight.
Sow in autumn and leave overwinter in cold frame
