skimmia
=========
               
skimmia
---------
This is a dense evergreen shrub, with aromatic foliage with a fine texture and fragrance. The flowers are tiny and white, and will appear in early spring to late spring, and are followed by bright red, decorative berries. They will grow to a height and spread of about 5ft.  They prefer a partially shaded setting with moist soil.
