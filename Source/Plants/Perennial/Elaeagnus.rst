elaeagnus
-----------
A bushy vigorous shrub with glossy leaves that have large central ,splash of gold.  2.4*3m.  Small fragrant flowers in autumn. Evergreen and thrives best in sun

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: elaeagnus  - Family: Pungens - 'dicksonii'  - Variety:
