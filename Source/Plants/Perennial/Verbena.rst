Verbena
---------
Flowers same year from early sowing. Architectural bored plant. Tall stems. Grown as annuals in colder regions.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Verbena  - Family:   - Variety: Bonariensis

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Perennial  - Propagation: Seed  - Hardiness: Hardy H5-H1 (<-10)  - Flowering Season: Summer to autumn 
Sow indoors from Jan, 0.5cm deep. Place in coldframe. Keep moist. 21-35 days for germination.  Final transplant 45cm apart when frost is over.
