osmanthus
===========
               
Sweet olive, Devilwood, Chinese holly
---------------------------------------
This species is grown for its profusion of small, very fragrant flowers they will appear in late spring, It will produce a dense, rounded habit with glossy dark green foliage.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Sweet olive, Devilwood, Chinese holly  - Family: burkwoodii  - Variety:
