sedum
=======
               
sedum, ice plant
------------------
clumps of upright stems with colourful, succulent leaves in spring, blossoming into masses of long-lasting flowers in late summer and autumn

loved by bees and butterflies

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: sedum, ice plant  - Family: Hylotelephium  - Variety:
