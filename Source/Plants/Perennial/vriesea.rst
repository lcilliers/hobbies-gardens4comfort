Flaming sword
---------------
do not place in direct sunlight; water once a week in it hear and spray the flowers; fertilize every month; This species is epiphytic bromeliad with dense, funnel rosettes of strap-shaped purple tipped, dull green leaves striped pale purple.  In mid summer, there are spike-like racemes of red-yellow bracts and tubular, green flowers.
