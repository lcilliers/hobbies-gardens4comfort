Shrubby cinquefoil potetilla
------------------------------
This is a deciduous dense shrub which grows about 1m tall, In early summer to autumn it will produce 25mm wide in shades of yellow, orange and white. They will grow in any soil, in full sun or partial- shade.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Shrubby cinquefoil potetilla  - Family: fruticosa  - Variety:

Potentilla nepalensis Miss Wilmot
-----------------------------------
'Miss Willmott' is an herbaceous perennial forming a clump to 35cm in height, with dark green leaves composed of five broad leaflets. Branched stems bear loose clusters of dark-centred cherry-pink flowers 2.5cm in width

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Potentilla nepalensis Miss Wilmot  - Family: Rosaceae  - Variety: nepalensis
