Chile rhubarb, Giant rhubarb
------------------------------
Bought 15cm plant 16/5/2001, targeted to be planted in woodlands

Giant leafed waterside subject for large areas. Its inflorenscences appear in early summer. Establish in spring and cover crowns in winter with it dead leaves as frost protection. It is not fully hardy. 3m H&W
This plant was accidently detroyed when I enlarged the bed
