hebe

hebe
------
This is a low growing frost-hardy shrub about 1m in height with thick narrowly glossy green leaves. The flowers are produced in spike of violet-purple flowers in the summer.

(bought a H. Wiri Charm from Long acres (cultivated in france  - cannot find the plant in any book, but it looks similar to the H. La Seduisante)

hebe
------
This species of shrub will produce oval to obovate dark green leaves. The flowers are borne in the summer with medium-sized white blooms in dense axillary racemes.
