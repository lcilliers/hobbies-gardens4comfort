clematis yellow bells shaped
------------------------------


A deciduous climbing shrub native to Western China.  Mid green leaves are lance-shaped to ovate. Single, yellow, bell-shaped flowers are born in mid summer and flower into autumn which are followed by decorative, silky seed heads. Dislikes dry conditions.


Classification
----------------



 - Common Names: clematis yellow bells shaped 
 - Family: tangutica 
 - Variety:
