Knautia
=========
               
Knautia macedonica Macedonian scabious
----------------------------------------
Knautia can be annuals or perennials, and may have simple or pinnately lobed leaves and scabious-like flower-heads

A clump-forming perennial to 80cm in height, with pinnately-lobed, dark green basal leaves to 8cm long, and simple or lobed stem leaves, with a succession of deep crimson pincushion-like flowerheads 3-4cm across, from mid to late summer

-------------------
