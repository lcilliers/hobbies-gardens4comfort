dock
------
The Rumex Sanguineus (bloody dock, red-veined dock) is a tap-rooted rosette forming perennial with long lance shaped dark green leaves with deep red veins. Grow to 90*30 cm
A member of the Dock family; it grows wild in Northern Europe.
