dictamnus
===========
               
dictamnus, candle plant
-------------------------
An erect perennial to 1.2m tall, with aromatic, dark green leaves divided into lance-shaped leaflets, and long racemes of white or purple flowers 2.5cm across in early summer

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: dictamnus, candle plant  - Family: Rutaceae  - Variety: albus purpureus

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Perennial  - Propagation: Seed  - Hardiness: Tender H7 - H10 (+0)  - Flowering Season:  
seed, full sun to partial shade.
