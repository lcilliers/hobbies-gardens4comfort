Berkheya
==========
               
purple berkeya South African Thistle
--------------------------------------

A perennial thistle, originating from South Africa, with large purplish-white flowers and jagged, silvery-grey leaves. Good for dry, sunny sites

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: purple berkeya South African Thistle 
 - Family:  
 - Variety: purpurea

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Perennial 
 - Propagation: Seed 
 - Hardiness: Hardy H5-H1 (<-10) 
 - Flowering Season: Summer to autumn 

white, purple .75m full sun
sow in late spring
Plant Berkheya in fertile, well drained soil in full sun. This plant will prefer a sheltered position where protection from severe winter weather is offered.
