berberis
==========
               
Barberry
----------

This is an evergreen hybrid it produced arching branches with crowded wooded stems, it will grow about 2.5m high and 3m wide, it has narrow deep green leaves. The flowers are golden yellow and appear in the spring. These are followed by blue-purple berries

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Barberry 
 - Family: stenophylla hybrid
