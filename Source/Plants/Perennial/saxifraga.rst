Saxifraga arendsii
--------------------
Saxifraga produces deep pink petite flowers with evergreen foliage. An easy to grow perennial they are perfect for adding colour to rockeries or patio containers but are a popular choice as ground cover at the front of borders due to its creeping nature. Prolific rosy pink blooms flower during the summer months from June to September and reach a maximum height of 15cm.

saxifraga rockfoil
--------------------
Saxifraga is the largest genus in the family Saxifragaceae, containing about 465 species of holarctic perennial plants, known as saxifrages or rockfoils. The Latin word saxifraga means literally "stone-breaker", from Latin saxum + frangere.
