kivi fruit
------------

hardy perennial. Seed. Fl late summer. Climber. Vigorous and ornamental, ideal supported on a sunny wall. Jear shaped leaves and charming cluster of maderate frangrant 4cm flowers. Grow 3-4 plants close together to produce kiwi fruit.
This is a fruit-bearing vine that will grow very fast.  It is an attractive climber for problem areas in your garden. It will grow in lots of soil conditions.  It can also be trained to cover fences or walls. You will need to provide sturdy support as they can grow to a length of 10m.

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Climber 
 - Propagation: Seed 
 - Hardiness: Semi Hardy H6 (-10) 
 - Flowering Season: Mid summer 

sow in well drained compost, takes 21-60 days to germinate. Transplant and grow on - plant out following spring.
Space plants 2 m apart
plant in full sun or part shade in ordinary garden soil
