phlox
=======
               
Annual phlox, Drummond phlox
------------------------------
This is a half-hardy annual, with flowers borne in clusters. This is a popular garden bedding plant and has long been appreciated for its brightly coloured flowering clusters which bloom in various colours..

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Annual phlox, Drummond phlox  - Family: drummondii  - Variety:

Spotted phlox, Wild sweet William, Meadow phlox
-------------------------------------------------
This is an upright perennial, it produces pink or lavender flowers with oval-leaved foliage. It will grow to a height of 3ft and will make a good choice for the middle of the perennial border.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Spotted phlox, Wild sweet William, Meadow phlox  - Family: paniculata  - Variety:
