senecio
=========
               
solver dust, silver ragwort
-----------------------------
mound forming, evergreen shrub or sub shrub usually grown as an annual. Loose corymbs of mustard yellow flowerheads, mid summer, in the second year after sowing

 It works particularly well with bold-coloured plants, with which its foliage contrasts well

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: solver dust, silver ragwort  - Family: cinereria  - Variety:
