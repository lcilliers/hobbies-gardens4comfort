Leucanthemum
==============
               
ox eye daisy
--------------
Leucanthemum are robust herbaceous perennials with narrowly oblong leaves and daisy-like flower-heads with white rays

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: ox eye daisy  - Family: Asteraceae  - Variety: vulgare
