glechoma
==========
               
ground ivy/alehoof
--------------------
syn nepeta glechoma, nepeta hederacea
good ground cover, may become invasive
good for baskets or window boxes
Fully hardy: the plant can tolerate temperatures down to about -15C or 5F.
Grows in damp waste ground, hedgerows and woodland margins.
