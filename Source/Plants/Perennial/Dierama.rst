Angel's fishing rod, Wandflower, African hairbell
---------------------------------------------------
An upright South African summer-flowering corm.  Bears evergreen, strap-like foliage.  Arching stems bear long tassels of tubular or bell-shaped deep pink, red or purple-white flowers.  ‘Album’ has white flowers. It reaches about 2m in height; spreads .25-.5m
Frost hardy: the plant can tolerate temperatures down to about -5C or 23F.
Dies down partially in winter.
