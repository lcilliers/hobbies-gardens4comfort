rambling rose white
---------------------
a vigorous rabling rose, introduced in 1900. It will grow to 5 m, In early to mid-summer, it produces masses of double, rosette, creamy white scented flowers. The leaves are glossy and often persist through the winter. Tollerates some shade.
Rambling roses are usually large and rampant, with a single, generally spectacular flush of flowers around mid summer.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: rambling rose white  - Family: alberic barbier  - Variety:

Hybrid tea rose bright red
----------------------------
a climbing large-flowered (hybrid tea) rose, introduced in 1964. It will grow to 3m or more high and 2.4 m across. Double, frgrant, bright red flowers are produced in profusion from early summer to early autumn. Tollerates some shade and is disease-resistant.

syn spanish beauty
--------------------
a vigorous climbing rose, introduced in 1927. It will grow to 6m and 4m across. In early summer fully doubled, rounded, sweetly scented, warm pink flowers with slightly frilled petals are borne in profusion in hanging clusters. Flowers only once. Has large showy hips that redden in autumn

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: syn spanish beauty  - Family: Madame gregoire staechelin  - Variety:

rose woodsii
--------------
this appears to be one of the wild roses, introduced from NA in 1888. Woodsii forms a graceful shrub of upright habit. The flowers are borne on thin, pliable stems formed in the previous year. It autumn small, pearshaped hips become bright red and last well into the winter

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: rose woodsii  - Family: woodsii  - Variety:
