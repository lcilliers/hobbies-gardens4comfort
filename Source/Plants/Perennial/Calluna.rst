heather highland rose
-----------------------

this plant produces long spikes of deep rose pink flowers in early autumn on golden bronze foliage. Heigt 45cm, spread 60cm
full sun or partial shade
incorporate moist peat, requires lime free soil
trim to base of flower spikes after flowering

heather dark star
-------------------

lime free soil, full sun or partial shade, mix moist moss peat with existing soil before planting deeply, firm slighly. Water well for fisrt spring and summer after planting.
Trim to base of flower spikes after flowering
