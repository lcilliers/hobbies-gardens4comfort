pulmonaria
============
               
Jerusalem sage
----------------
evergreen perennial
This is an evergreen perennial, the leaves are heavily spotted and hairy, and about 25cm, The flowers bloom in white and shades of pink and blue.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Jerusalem sage  - Family: saccharata  - Variety:
