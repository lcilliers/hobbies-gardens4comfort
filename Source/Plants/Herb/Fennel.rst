Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Herb 
 - Propagation: Seed 
 - Hardiness: Tender H7 - H10 (+0) 
 - Flowering Season: Summer to autumn 

Sow March to June 1.5mm deep. Usually appears 7-21 days.  As bulbs start to swell build the earth up around them. Apply liquid fertilizer every two weeks

2024-03-17: sow seed from Verve in pods.  Should try to sow this direct also
