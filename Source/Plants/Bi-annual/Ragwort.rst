Ragwort
=========

Ragwort ( Jacobaea vulgaris) is a very common plant that is native to Eurasia that is generally found in dry, open places. Ragwort is native to the UK and provides a home and food source to at least 77 insect species. The main problem with ragwort is that it is poisonous to animals, in particular, horses and cattle.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Ragwort 
 - Family: Jacobaea 
 - Variety: vulgaris
