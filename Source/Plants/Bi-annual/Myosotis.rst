Myosotis
==========
               
Alpine forget-me-not, Forget me not
-------------------------------------

This genus consists of annuals and perennials, there are about 50 or so species, The flowers are small and blue, sometimes they are pink or white and will bloom in the spring.
This is Hardy Biennial or perennial it is low and clump-forming,  It will prefers full sun to partial shade and a rich, moist soil. It will produce prolific blooms of the most charming blue surrounded by light green to bright foliage.

'Blue Ball' is a compact rounded annual or biennial to 15cm, with dense cymes of bright bue flowers in spring and early summer

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Alpine forget-me-not, Forget me not 
 - Family: Boraginaceae 
 - Variety: alpestris
