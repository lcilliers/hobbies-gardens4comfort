Oenothera odorata
===================
               
Evening Primrose
------------------
Oenothera can be annuals, biennials or perennials, upright or spreading in habit, with simple or lobed leaves and bowl-shaped, white, yellow or pink flowers over a long period in summer

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Bi-annual  - Propagation: Seed  - Hardiness: Tender H7 - H10 (+0)  - Flowering Season: Mid summer 
Grow in poor to moderately fertile, well-drained soil in full sun. Propagate by seed sown in early summer
Cut back after flowering
