eranthis
==========
               
Winter aconite
----------------
A small woodland tuberous perennial, 10cm high, bearing yellow buttercup-like flowers, surrounded by leafy bracts, from mid to late winter. Prefers alkaline or neutral soil

Classification
----------------

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Bulb  - Propagation: Seed  - Hardiness: Hardy H5-H1 (<-10)  - Flowering Season: Late winter 
Yellow .15m  full sun, semi shade
Plant Eranthis seeds in a cold frame in spring.
Plant Eranthis tubers in late summer before they shrivel.
Grow in a humus-rich, moderately fertile soil that doesn't dry out. Tolerates most soil types but does best in alkaline soils
Dry tubers may not establish well
