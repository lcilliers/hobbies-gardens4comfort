leucojum
==========
               
snowflake
-----------
This is a small spring-flowering bulb, The leaves are blue-green long and strap-shaped and slender. The flowers have 6 white, green-spotted petals they bloom in late spring and early summer and are fragrant. They prefer soil which is rich in organic matter, moist and well drained with full sun or partial shade.
