narcissus daffodil tete-a-tete
--------------------------------
Daffodil Tete-a-Tete is a dwarf daffodil variety with deep golden yellow flowers. It grows to 10-15cm high and produces up to 3 blooms per stem. It blooms in early spring and has oval petals and a long crown. It is suitable for planting in pots, borders, rock gardens or grass. It is hardy and can tolerate some exposure and partial shade

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: narcissus daffodil tete-a-tete  - Family:   - Variety: Tete-a-tete

daffodil petti hoop
---------------------
A hoop petticoat daffodil is a type of flowering plant in the family Amaryllidaceae, native to some parts of Europe. It has a distinctive shape with a large, funnel-shaped trumpet and very narrow petals. It is also known as Narcissus bulbocodium or petticoat daffodil

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: daffodil petti hoop  - Family: Amaryllidaceae  - Variety: daffodil pettyhoop

daffodil St Keverne
---------------------
Daffodil St Keverne is a variety of daffodil that belongs to the trumpet group, meaning it has one flower per stem with a large, flared cup. It has bright yellow petals and a deeper gold cup. It grows up to 45 cm tall and blooms in mid-spring
