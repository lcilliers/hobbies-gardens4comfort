Muhlenbergia
==============
               
Mist Grass, Pink Hairy Grass
------------------------------
The most well known member of the genus is the pink Muhly Grass. With dramatic arching plumes of soft pink flowers, large plant, forms an arching clump of fine glue-green leaves throughout the summer, turning copper during autumn.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Mist Grass, Pink Hairy Grass  - Family: Poaceae  - Variety: Capillaris

Propagation Notes
-------------------

 - Plant Type: Grass  - Propagation: Seed  - Hardiness: Hardy H5-H1 (<-10)  - Flowering Season: Late summer 
Persists well throughout winter.  Thrives in full sun or partial shade.
