Stipa
=======
               
Pony tails, Stipa, Feather grass
----------------------------------
Compact perennial grass. Stipa can be evergreen or deciduous, mostly perennial grasses with arching, folded or rolled, linear leaves and narrow panicles of often long-awned spikelets in summer and autumn

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Pony tails, Stipa, Feather grass  - Family: poaceae  - Variety: tenuissima

Golden Oats, Giant Oats
-------------------------
Stipa can be evergreen or deciduous, mostly perennial grasses with arching, folded or rolled, linear leaves and narrow panicles of often long-awned spikelets in summer and autumn

S. gigantea is a robust tufted evergreen grass to 2m, with arching linear green leaves and large panicles of oat-like, long-awned purplish flowers which ripen to gold

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Golden Oats, Giant Oats  - Family: Poaceae  - Variety: gigantea
