dactylis
==========
               
orchard grass cat grass
-------------------------
Dactylis glomerata, also known as cock's-foot, orchard grass, or cat grass is a common species of grass in the genus Dactylis. It is a cool-season perennial C₃ bunchgrass native throughout most of Europe, temperate Asia, and northern Africa.
