dryopteris
============
               
male fern, wood fern, buckler fern
------------------------------------
Perfect for any shade planting, these ferns are easy to grow and many will cope with dry shade once established. An excellent foil to other plants, they have a quiet charm all of their own when the fronds unfurl in spring to reveal the fresh, divided foliage. There are many attractive forms available.

Some is evergreen and semi evergreen, others are deciduous.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: male fern, wood fern, buckler fern  - Family: filix-mas  - Variety:

Dryopteris Wallichiana Fern
-----------------------------
A deciduous or semi-evergreen fern, forming tufts of erect, lance-shaped, bipinnatifid fronds, bright yellowish-green when young, clothed with blackish-brown scales on the stalk and midrib

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Dryopteris Wallichiana Fern  - Family:   - Variety: Wallichiana
