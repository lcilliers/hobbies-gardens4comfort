athyrium
==========
               
Auriculate Lady, Eared Lady
-----------------------------
The color and texture of the fronds of this fern are really neat. The rachis of this fern are a beautiful purpleish-deep-red and a lovely contrast to the pale green leaves.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: Auriculate Lady, Eared Lady  - Family: otophorum  - Variety:
