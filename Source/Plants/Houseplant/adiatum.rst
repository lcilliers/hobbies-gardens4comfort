adiatum
=========
               
maidenhair fern
-----------------
This species is an evergreen fern with leaves that are broad and triangular. They are medium green in colour. They are from central America. Frost tender: the plant can be damaged by temperatures below 5C or 41F.  Remove fading fronds regularly.

Classification
----------------

 - Common Names: maidenhair fern  - Family: tenerum  - Variety:
