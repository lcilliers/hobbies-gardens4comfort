Kalanchoe
===========
               
Good luck plant, Flaming Katy
-------------------------------
This genus consists of about 150 species of perennial succulents or shrubs with very fleshy, mainly cylindrical, oval or linear leaves and bell-shaped to tubular flowers which will bloom either white, yellow, orange-brown, red-purple. They are frost tender, min 7-15C, and will need full sun or partial shade with well drained soil. You should keep them moist from spring to autumn. Propagation is by seed, offsets or stem cuttings in the spring or summer.
