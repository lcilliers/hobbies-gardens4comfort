About This Site
---------------

This website contains **2,546 photographs** documenting plants in my gardens over the years, along with detailed notes on cultivation, propagation, and maintenance. It's an ever-evolving personal reference that I continue to refine and expand.

The content reflects real-world experience in UK gardens - what works, what doesn't, and the continuous learning process that makes gardening so rewarding. Whether you're looking for specific plant information or inspiration for your own garden, I hope you find something valuable here.
