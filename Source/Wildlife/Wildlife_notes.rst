Wildlife
==========
2021-02-08: Decided to buy a wild life camera to get more information on the fox and what is living in the hole next to the pond.  Experimenting with the camera turns out to be great fun - except that this week it is bitterly cold
2002-05-12: We found a hare in our garden. It is burrowing under a Spirrea bush (and I am worried that it is going to kill the bush. Is is also quite cute in the back garden mulching away on the lawn. It is however slowly starting to get on my nerves. It found the vegetable garden last night - nothing is left.
1999-05-02: We have two wild ducks that decided to use our pond, and is breeding somewhere close by. I am not sure where it is that they are breeding but hope that cats will not find them.
