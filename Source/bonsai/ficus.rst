Ficus fig
-----------Figs can be readily grown in the UK climate. They were used as a sweetening method before sugar was more widely used. Fig trees grow well in UK gardens and are such a versatile fruit to have at your fingertips. Figs can be enjoyed in summer salads, or wrapped in prosciutto, roasted or grilled, and eaten with cheeses
2024-01-30: Two ficus bonsai trees have grown well through 2023 and overwintered in the greenhouse.

2023-03-18: Reshaped the figs in pots to allow them to become more bushy.
