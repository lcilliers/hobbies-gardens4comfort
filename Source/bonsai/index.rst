Propagating trees
-----------------

Sowing steps
soak for 1 night
next day soak compost disk, place in pot
sow seed thinly, cover with compost
pot each pot in bag, pierce bag
put in green for 2-3 weeks, then in fridge for 4-6 weeks
put in greenhouse until germination
when germinated, remove from bag
